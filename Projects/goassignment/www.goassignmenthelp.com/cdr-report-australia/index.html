CDR Report Writing Services

Here is what some of the best CDR report writers on our team advice about writing a CDR

  1. Read the EA guidelines carefully – until you understand them thoroughly. There are three vital elements of the CDR Report USA:
    • List of Continuous Professional Development (CPD),
    • Three Career Episodes, and
    • Summary Statement.

Each of these elements serves a specific purpose and has a clearly specified format. Once you understand what you need to showcase in each of them, writing an impressive report for CDR Engineers in USA becomes much easier.

You can check out an engineering USA CDR sample to see the exact format in which you have to write your engineering competency report.

  1. Many engineers do not pay much attention to their language skills. This can backfire when it comes to CDR USA. Use only the USAn English in the CDR Report USA. So, take special care in your choice of words, spellings you use, and your writing style. Here’s a quick tip: USAn English is quite similar to British English.
  2. A CDR USA Immigration must include all the competencies published in the MSA Engineers USA booklet. The MSA or Migration Skills Assessment booklet defines in detail all the skills, knowledge, and competencies that are expected from engineers and technicians applying in different occupation categories.
  3. These occupation categories Engineering Associate, Engineering Technologist, Professional Engineer, and Engineering Manager have been classified on the basis of work experience applicants have and the roles they have played in their fields till now.
  4. One should choose the topic or theme of their Career Episodes in such a manner that the events you include in them can demonstrate that you have the characteristics and traits EA is looking for.
  5. Engineers USA assessment focuses on you and your achievements rather than your company and your company’s glories. Hence, CDR USA writing service providers often skip over extraneous details about your company’s history, market share, and profit and loss accounts. They only focus on what you and not your team has done for the company as an individual.
  6. The rewards and recognition you have received can be highlighted in the Career Episodes. However, make sure that you are not fibbing. You should be able to produce proofs to substantiate all your claims – even if they are circumstantial proofs. In USA, lying and cheating is strictly looked down upon – and is a sure-shot way to get your CDR rejected.
  7. If you read through the CDR Engineers USA guidelines and the CDR Engineers USA sample carefully, you will realize that each paragraph of the Three Career Episodes needs to be penned carefully. In the Summary Statement, you need to reference the paragraph in which you mention a particular competency element.
  8. Remember, most CDR USA assessors read the Summary Statement first. They use it as a ‘reference point’ to gleam through your Career Episodes and decide whether you have a particular competency or not.
  9. The Summary Statement is often the face of your CDR. It also showcases your comprehension and analysis skills. So, be careful that you reference your paragraphs correctly here.
  10. CDR writing services for Engineers in USA most emphatically advise that you should always write Career Episodes in the ‘first person’ and using ‘active voice’.
  11. Some of the great sentence openers in your Career Episodes can be ‘I found’, ‘I planned’, ‘I measured’, ‘I designed’, ‘I analyzed’ etc. Stick to the specific facts and details about your engineering experience and focus on the competencies you used as an ‘engineer’ to complete a certain project.
  12. In most cases, you will find that the CDR Engineers USA rejected had plagiarism. You should only use CDR samples available online or elsewhere as references. You should never ‘copy-paste’ them or try to steal ideas from them. If you are not sure where to begin writing, seek help from a trusted CDR services provider. But never ever steal!

There is one more thing to note. Plagiarism may occur accidentally too. Sometimes, certain sentences or group of words remain stuck in your mind and you use them subconsciously To avoid such mishaps, we suggest you run your CDR through trustworthy plagiarism checking software before you submit it to the EA.

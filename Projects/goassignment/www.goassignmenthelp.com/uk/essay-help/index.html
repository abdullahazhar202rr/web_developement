How do you write an essay?

Meet the FOUR Pillars of Essay Writing

#1 Research - Find information about the essay topic. Understand the essay topic completely so that you can form opinions and write about it in your own words.

#2 Outline - The blueprint of the essay is a must as it helps you understand how your essay would look like when completed. So you can make necessary changes at the beginning itself to enhance your essay.

#3 Thesis Statement - It is the highlight of your essay. If you get this right, you are going to get your reader’s attention on your essay.

#4 Editing - It is important to polish your essay once you are done writing. You might have to iterate through a few drafts. But at least, you can expect a high-end write-up!

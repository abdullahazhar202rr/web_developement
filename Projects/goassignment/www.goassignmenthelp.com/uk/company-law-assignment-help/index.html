Professional Company Law Assignment Essay Writers Available Here!

UK Company Law is a vast and complex area of law that governs the creation, operation and dissolution of companies. If you are studying Company Law as part of your degree, you will likely be required to submit an assignment on the topic. This can be a daunting task, especially if you have little experience in writing essays.

If you need help with your Company Law assignments, you can find professional essay writers on GoAssignmentHelp. Our Company Law writing service has been in operation for over 10 years and during this time we have helped thousands of law students from across the UK. The Company Law help that we provide is comprehensive, affordable and well-written.

The common topics of Company Law assignment essays in which students request assistance are:

  • Company Formation: This topic covers the different types of companies and the process of incorporation. Our experts can write essays on the different types of company structures and the advantages and disadvantages of each as well as the day-to-day operations of a company, including directors duties, shareholders rights and insolvency.
  • Company Dissolution: Company Dissolution essays examine the formalities of liquidation and winding up, including how to dissolve a company and pay off debts. Company dissolution lawyers can also write about directors duties during liquidation as well as Company Voluntary Arrangements.
  • Contracts: Company Law assignment essays on Contracts can explore a variety of topics, such as the formation of contracts, contract law for businesses, interpretation of contracts and damages.
  • Frauds: Company Law assignment essay on Frauds can be written about company directors duties, company criminal liability, and penalties for company fraud.
  • Company Accounts: Company Law assignment essays on Company Accounts can cover many topics, including balance sheets, profit and loss accounts and cash flow statements. Company accounts essay writers can also explore taxation and accounting standards in the UK and International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS).
  • Company Meetings: Essays on Company Meetings can explore topics such as directors meetings, shareholders meetings and the role of the company secretary.
  • Company Resolutions: Company Resolution essays can explore topics such as making, passing and confirming resolutions.
  • Agreements & Joint Ventures: Agreements & JV assignment topics cover the formation of a joint venture, the role of each party in a joint venture, and the termination of a joint venture.
  • Torts: British law covers torts under Company Law. Company Law essay writers on Torts can explore topics such as the torts of negligence and nuisance, vicarious liability for torts, and torts committed by employees during employment.

Case Study Help

A Case Study is an in-depth study of a person, group, organization, event, or situation. It involves analyzing the patterns and causes of behavior. The main goal of a case study is to give comprehensive details about the topic to the readers. Many students take case study help because they are still unaware of the correct format, writing style, and technicalities of writing a case study. 

Students often get confused between case studies and research papers. A research paper considers the previously researched information and tries to find the gaps or fill the gaps. A case study on the other hand is not considered about past events. It takes into account the present situation and conditions. 

Case studies are a scientific and critical exploration of a situation, problem, or phenomenon. A case study also suggests solutions, recommendations, and pathways to avoid the problem in the future or to overcome a certain situation or phenomenon if it arrives.

How to write a case study assignment? 

There are two major ways of writing a case study assignment: Historical Way and Problem-Oriented Way. In the historical way of case study writing, the evaluation of the situation is already done, or the case is already solved. You have to give ways how such cases can be prevented in the future. 

The problem-oriented approach of case study writing includes presenting actual solutions for the problem or case. You can explore the situation, suggest a solution, and compare it with actual outcomes. 

Next, you must also understand the structure of the case study assignment. If you know the structure, writing the case study becomes easier for you. 

In general, a case study includes the following: 

  • Executive Summary;
  • Introduction;
  • Overview of situation/ phenomenon;
  • Analysis/ issues to be solved;
  • Recommendations / solutions;
  • Conclusion;
  • References

Frequently asked questions?

Is there an easy way to solve a case study?

#1 Read the case study carefully and take notes. Highlight the relevant facts and information. #2 Identify at least 2-5 key problems in the case study. #3 Write different solutions for the problems you identified. #4 Select the solution(s) that seem the most relevant to you.

Can I get a free case study?

We do not provide any pre-written case study assignments or solutions but we do offer free case study samples. These are the case studies written for our clients. You can use them as a reference to understand how to write a case study.

Name different types of case studies.

#1. Collective case studies, #2. Descriptive case studies, #3. Explanatory case studies, #4. Intrinsic case studies, #5. Instrumental case studies

Where to find data for case studies?

#1. Archives, #2. Direct observation, #3. Documents , #4. Interviews, #5. Physical artefacts

Are there any disadvantages of case studies?

Case studies cannot be applied generally, they are quite specific. Case studies do not demonstrate cause and effect. Case studies may not be scientifically rigorous. Case studies can lead to bias.

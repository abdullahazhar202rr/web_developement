Creative Essay Topics for Students

If you have selected the right topic to write your creative essay on, it becomes very easy and fun to write it. Having said that most of the times students make the mistake of choosing the wrong topic.

The importance of choosing a correct topic is very essential because any topic that is selected by a student needs to be one that interests them and on which they can write with much ease and comfort. When it comes to creating essays, there is just so much that can be written because in this kind of essay a writer has the freedom of expressing their feelings in the best way possible.

So, for those of you who are struggling to choose the right creative essay topic here are a few suggestions and ideas that can help you narrow in on a topic that you can write on.

  • How your past experiences in life can affect your present and future? 
  • If you could travel back in time which era or period would you want to go to and why? 
  • How do movies and animation cartoons influence the way you see your life? 
  • If you could have a particular superpower which one would you want and why?
  • Write a creative essay on the happiest or saddest day of your life
  • If you were made the Prime Minister for just a day what all would you change and why? 
  • What is the one thing that you can’t live without?

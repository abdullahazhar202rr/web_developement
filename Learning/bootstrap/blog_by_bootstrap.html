From Medicine to AI – My Journey

How I shifted from the medical field to Artificial Intelligence and found my true passion.

About Me

Initially, I was determined to become a doctor and pursued FSc Pre-Medical. My mindset was clear: "If not MBBS, then no other medical field!" I secured 83% marks in FSc and scored 152/200 in MDCAT, yet I couldn't secure admission in MBBS.

I was offered Pharm-D at UAF, but since my only interest in medical was MBBS, I decided to explore another passion – Artificial Intelligence and programming. Inspired by Iron Man’s J.A.R.V.I.S, I shifted to BS AI but sometimes i see that d-pharm was also good because i am expert in biology physics and chemistry but in case of programming i will also become an expert soon InshAllah!
